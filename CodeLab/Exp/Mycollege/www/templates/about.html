Thiagarajar College, founded on 7th July 1949, was formally inaugurated on 12th October 1949 by His Excellency, the King of Bhavnagar, the then Governor of Madras State. At the inaugural function, the founder, Karumuttu Thiagarajan Chettiar, described this institution as a "small and humble endeavour in education". The college, now has an impressive history covering 65 epoch-making years. This is an Autonomous Institution Affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University. Re-accredited with "A" Grade by NAAC in the 3rd Cycle with a CGPA of 3.37.

Vision
